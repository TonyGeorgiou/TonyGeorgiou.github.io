FC Gallery:

"Make room for art!"

The FC Gallery exhibits fully framed, limited edition copies of original fine art collages. Please enjoy! They are for your sheer aesthetic perusal and for sale.

Art is not just for gallery-goers.
Our mission is to add gems of artistic delight to your home. As collages they may be small, but their inventiveness (and quirky humour!) will add an extra dimension to any room.

The collage of your choice can be sent to you as a limited edition print, in it's original size, fully framed as shown,or unframed if required.

Collage may be one of the new kids on the block in the long history of art, but its impact on visual communication has been immense. Ever since Picasso stuck a little department store label onto a piece of ochre-coloured paper, painted on it and sketched over it extending his lines of ink beyond the label's bounds, collage has stuck itself deeply onto our aesthetic taste.
